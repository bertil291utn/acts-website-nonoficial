### OUR VISION

Acts29 aspires to be an international multicultural mission movement of young people, whose lives have been disciplined and transformed for missional leadership in the Church and in the world.

### WE ARE

**Discipleship School**  
We participate in the process of shaping lives in community as we follow Jesus. We do not merely impart knowledge.

*(Imagen asociada en web: “Chocar los cinco” / archivo en sitio)*

**Leadership School**  
We entrust young people with delegated leadership. We mentor and empower them to become servant leaders.

**Mission School**  
We encourage young people to participate in God’s mission of reconciling all things to himself.

*(Imagen asociada en web: “Construcción de casas” / archivo en sitio)*

### WE CELEBRATE — 20 YEARS OF STORY

We celebrate God’s faithfulness through schools that transform lives. Each year, we run different schools designed to impact every student and the nations they reach.

### Historia del movimiento

The movement began as a partnership between the Iglesia del Pacto Evangélico del Ecuador and the Mission Covenant Church of Sweden. The first school was held in Montecristi, Ecuador in 2004. Since then Chile, Democratic Republic of Congo, Republic of Congo, Finland, India, Mexico, Russia, Spain, Sweden, China and Thailand have held schools while additional nations have sent participants.

## Página: Ecuador 2026 (`/ecuador`)

Título: **Ecuador 2026 | Acts29**

- ecuador 2026  
- If your heart beats for missions and you long to see lives transformed, this is your moment.  
- Join Acts29 School 2026 and be part of what God is doing.  
- **New Adventures in the Andes Mountains and the Amazon**  

Ecuador has hosted Hechos29 School 13 times since its founding in 2004, making it the most iconic destination of the program and we truly love Ecuador! Since the early 1970s, Equmenia has partnered with the Iglesia del Pacto Evangélico del Ecuador (IPEE), a church with congregations throughout the country. Together, we share a passion for missions and a desire to strengthen local communities. During the outreach weeks, we will visit several of these congregations, serving and learning alongside the school team.

### The School

The school will start at the end of August 2026. The Nordic participants and leaders will start on August 17th with a few introductory days at Karlskoga Folkhögskola. After that, we will travel together to Quito and from there to the school's base. The final location of the base is still to be confirmed, but we are considering several incredible places in Ecuador that will provide the perfect setting for this experience.

### A School for the World

Acts29 is an international and multicultural discipleship school. We invite young people from different countries to share this journey together. You will live in community with approximately 40 participants, typically representing between 5 and 7 nationalities. We will also receive teachers from various countries, further enriching the training experience.

### Days at the Base will be Filled With:

- Community and team life  
- Biblical teaching  
- Practical workshops  
- Devotionals  
- Times of prayer  
- Activities and recreation  

On weekends, you will be part of a team traveling to serve in local churches in partnership with the Iglesia del Pacto Evangélico del Ecuador (IPEE). In addition, once a month we will have a full week of intensive missions in the Ecuadorian Highlands and the Amazon region, where we will serve, learn, and experience everyday life alongside Ecuadorian families.

### Short Facts

- **Dates:** August 29 – November 22, 2026  
- **Location:** Ecuador (Highlands and Amazon region)  
- **Cost:** $2100.00 USD  
- **Languages:** English and Spanish (full interpretation provided; Spanish proficiency is not required).  
- **Application Deadline:** July 31, 2026  

### These Months Will Be a Time To:

- Deepen your relationship with God  
- Prepare biblically and practically for missions  
- Serve in the field and in God’s work  
- Impact lives with the message of Jesus  

This is not just a school… it is an experience that can shape your purpose forever.
